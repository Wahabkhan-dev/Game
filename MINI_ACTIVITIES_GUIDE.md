## 🛣️ LEVEL 2 — The Road Home (Gleeda's Trust Arc)

Level 2 features **7 trust-building mini-activities** that unlock Gemma's cage through emotional bonding.

### **Main Gameplay: L2_RoadRunScene**
- **Type:** Platformer (collect items, dodge obstacles)
- **Duration:** ~8 minutes
- **Objective:** Navigate to Gemma's new location
- **Story:** Gleeda pursues Gemma through jungle roads

### **🔓 Cage Unlock Mini-Games Chain** (Unlocked sequentially)

#### **1. L2_CalmerScene** 🧘
- **When:** First approach to cage
- **Activity:** Soothing meditation/calm gesture
- **Mechanics:** Hold still, breathe with the puppy
- **Purpose:** Reduce stress, build initial connection
- **Output:** Trust 0% → 25%

#### **2. L2_FeedScene** 🍖
- **Activity:** Drag & drop safe vs toxic foods
- **Safe Foods:** Meat, bone, chicken, cheese
- **Toxic Foods:** Chocolate 🚫, grapes 🚫, candy 🚫, mushroom 🚫
- **Mechanics:** Drag 4 safe foods to bowl, avoid 2 toxic ones
- **Time Limit:** 30 seconds
- **Lives:** 2 mistakes allowed
- **Purpose:** Show care through proper nutrition
- **Output:** Trust 25% → 50%

#### **3. L2_PetScene** 🤚
- **Activity:** Gentle touch/petting interaction
- **Mechanics:** Tap/click on Gemma to pet
- **Visual:** Gemma responds positively
- **Purpose:** Physical bonding and comfort
- **Output:** Trust 50% → 60%

#### **4. L2_RhythmScene** 🎵
- **Activity:** Rhythm matching (4 beats)
- **Mechanics:** Tap ❤️ on the beat (950ms interval)
- **Visual:** Pulsing heart, beat ring animation
- **Success Condition:** Hit all 4 beats correctly
- **Purpose:** Emotional synchronization, full trust unlock
- **Output:** Trust 60% → 100%

#### **5. L2_CatchScene** 🎾
- **Activity:** Playful chase/catch game
- **Mechanics:** Catch falling objects or chase animations
- **Purpose:** Playful bonding, fun interaction
- **Timing:** Later in sequence for reinforcement

#### **6. L2_DodgeScene** 🏃
- **Activity:** Dodging obstacles while protecting Gemma
- **Mechanics:** Avoid hazards (snake, obstacles)
- **Purpose:** Protective instinct, staying vigilant
- **Challenge:** Enemies approach, must block them

#### **7. L2_FirefliesScene** ✨
- **Activity:** Collect magical fireflies
- **Mechanics:** Grab glowing objects
- **Purpose:** Wonder and distraction, calming atmosphere
- **Visual:** Glowing particles, soothing animation

#### **8. L2_MushroomScene** 🍄
- **Activity:** Platformer section (mushroom forest)
- **Mechanics:** Navigate springy mushroom terrain
- **Purpose:** Miniboss-like challenge before final unlock

### **L2_EndScene**
- **Type:** Cutscene + celebration
- **Display:** Gleeda and Gemma reunited, cage unlocked
- **Outcome:** Transition to Chapter 3 (hospital crisis)

## 🚨 LEVEL 7 — Final Trial (5-Stage Emergency Rescue)

**Total Duration:** ~20 minutes  
**Narrative:** One puppy is critical; midnight race to hospital

### **STAGE 1: Find the House Key** 🔑

#### **Gameplay: L7_Stage1Scene**
- **Type:** Platformer/puzzle exploration
- **Setting:** Dark home, power out
- **Objectives:**
  1. Restore power (fuse box puzzle)
  2. Unlock attic cabinet
  3. Search basement
  4. Assemble jeep key (3 fragments)

#### **Key Activities:**
- **Power Restoration** ⚡
  - Navigate to fuse box
  - Solve electrical puzzle
  - Activate lights (removes darkness overlay)

- **Fragment Collection** 🔑
  - Find 3 key fragments (exploration)
  - Assemble into complete key

- **Equipment Gathering** 🧰
  - Locate tools and supplies
  - Prepare for vehicle departure

### **STAGE 2: Repair the Tire** 🔧

#### **Gameplay: L7_Stage2Scene**
- **Type:** Multi-step activity puzzle
- **Setting:** Roadside in rain
- **Objective:** Fix flat tire to continue journey

#### **5 Tire Repair Tasks:**
1. **🔍 Locate Tools** — Find screwdriver, wrench, jack
2. **🔩 Loosen Lugnuts** — Unscrew wheel fasteners
3. **🔨 Apply Jack** — Lift vehicle carefully
4. **🛞 Rotate Tire** — Remove flat, install spare
5. **✅ Tighten & Test** — Secure wheel, test drive

**Mechanics:** Sequential unlocking, drag & drop interactions  
**Time:** Real-time, no excessive pressure  
**Story Impact:** Prepares for next stage

### **STAGE 3: Collect Fuel** ⛽

#### **Gameplay: L7_Stage3Scene**
- **Type:** 5-step puzzle chain
- **Setting:** Rainy gas station highway
- **Objective:** Complete all 5 fuel tasks to proceed

#### **5 Fuel Activities:**

**1. L7_S3_Activity1 — Move the Barrel** 🛢️
- **Type:** Drag & drop
- **Mechanics:** Drag barrel from pile onto green marker
- **Challenge:** Precision positioning (snap distance ~48px)
- **Feedback:** "Barrel placed!" on success
- **Time:** ~30 seconds per attempt

**2. L7_S3_Activity2 — Start the Generator** 🔌
- **Type:** Rapid tapping / hold button
- **Mechanics:** TAP "PULL" button rapidly (or SPACE key)
- **Visual:** Progress bar fills as you pull
- **Success Condition:** Reach 100% fill
- **Drain Rate:** -2.2 per tick if not pulling (penalty)
- **Time:** ~15 seconds of tapping
- **Feedback:** Generator powers on with visual/audio cue

**3. L7_S3_Activity3 — Connect the Pipes** 🔧
- **Type:** Rotation puzzle
- **Mechanics:** Click pipes to rotate them horizontal (straight)
- **Pieces:** 4 pipe segments
- **Goal:** All pipes aligned horizontally (0° or 180°)
- **Visual:** Flow lines appear when correct
- **Time:** ~20 seconds once all rotations complete

**4. L7_S3_Activity4 — Unlock the Tank** 🔒
- **Type:** Rotary code puzzle
- **Mechanics:** 
  - Dial has 10 positions (0–9)
  - Code is 3 digits long
  - Rotate to each digit, then press SET
  - Each correct digit shows ✓
  - Wrong digit causes camera shake + red flash
- **Time:** ~30 seconds for correct code
- **Feedback:** Sparkle burst on each correct digit

**5. L7_S3_Activity5 — Fill the Fuel** ⛽
- **Type:** Hold-to-pour game
- **Mechanics:**
  - Hold "POUR" button (or E key)
  - Fuel meter rises from 75% → 100%
  - Rate: +1.1% per tick while holding, -0.3% per tick if not
- **Duration:** ~45 seconds to full tank
- **Visual:** Meter fills with green color, percentage display
- **Feedback:** Flash + "Fuel Tank Full!" message

### **STAGE 4: Drive to the Hospital** 🚗

#### **Gameplay: L7_Stage4Scene**
- **Type:** Driving simulator with mini-games
- **Duration:** ~8 minutes
- **Distance:** 9.6 km to hospital
- **Health Mechanic:** Puppies' safety 0–100%

#### **Controls:**
- **Accelerate:** Right arrow / D key
- **Brake:** Left arrow / A key
- **Max Speed:** 6.4 units/frame
- **Acceleration:** 0.22 units/frame²

#### **5 Driving Hazard Mini-Games:**

**1. 🛑 Speed Breakers (4 total)**
- **Challenge:** Must brake BEFORE breaker
- **Safe Speed:** ≤1.7 units
- **Penalty:** Hit at high speed = -24% health + "Speed breaker!" message
- **Reward:** Smooth pass = "✅ Smooth!" + car bounce
- **Locations:** x = 1500, 2900, 5400, 8600

**2. 🚦 Traffic Lights (3 total)**
- **Challenge:** Stop at red, go at green
- **Duration:** Red light lasts 2.8 seconds, then turns green
- **Penalty:** Run red light = -24% health + "Ran the red!"
- **Reward:** Stop correctly = light turns green + "🟢 Green — go!"
- **Locations:** x = 2100, 4400, 7700

**3. 🚧 Road Blocks (2 total)**
- **Challenge:** Clear debris by rapid button mashing
- **Mechanic:** Hold "CLEAR" button to fill progress bar 0–100%
- **Time:** ~10 seconds of mashing
- **Reward:** Barrier disappears, road clears + "✅ Road cleared!"
- **Locations:** x = 3500, 6900

**4. ⚠️ Quick-Time Event (2 total)**
- **Challenge:** Swerve around fallen branch before timer runs out
- **Mechanic:** Tap "SWERVE" button before 1.5-second timer expires
- **Penalty:** Too slow = -24% health + "💥 Too slow!"
- **Reward:** Successful swerve = car tilts + "🌀 Swerved!"
- **Locations:** x = 4900, 8100

**5. ⛰️ Steep Climb (1 total)**
- **Challenge:** Hold gas pedal to power up the hill
- **Mechanic:** Hold "GAS" (D key or SPACE) to fill 0–100%
- **Rate:** +1.0% per tick while holding, -0.7% if not
- **Duration:** ~15 seconds of continuous holding
- **Reward:** Reach 100% → car crests hill + "⛰️ Over the top!"
- **Location:** x = 6200

### **Objective Checklist (All 6):**
- ✓ Mind the speed breakers
- ✓ Stop at red lights
- ✓ Clear road blocks
- ✓ Quick-time swerve
- ✓ Climb the hill
- ✓ Reach hospital

## 🎓 Design Patterns

### **Pattern 1: Emotional Progression**
- **Start:** Fear, danger, urgency
- **Build:** Trust, bonding, care
- **Peak:** Celebration, family
- **Climax:** Life-or-death rescue
- **Resolution:** Safe, healed, complete

### **Pattern 2: Skill Introduction**
- Drag & drop (easiest) → Rhythm (timing) → Platformer (reflex) → Driving (complex control)

### **Pattern 3: Mini-Game Chains**
- Multiple activities unlock sequentially
- Each builds on previous
- Final activity completes objective

### **Pattern 4: Time Pressure Scaling**
- **Early games:** Generous time (30+ seconds)
- **Mid games:** Real-time (15–20 seconds)
- **Late games:** Intense (sub-10 second windows)

## 💡 Usage Tips for Developers

### **When to Add a Mini-Game:**
✅ Major story beat (rescue, healing, celebration)  
✅ Significant emotion (fear, love, joy)  
✅ Mechanical break from platforming  
✅ Character bonding moment  

### **How to Structure:**
1. **Intro panel** (title + instructions)
2. **Success condition** (clear win state)
3. **Failure state** (retry or progress anyway)
4. **Feedback** (visual + audio + text confirmation)
5. **Story continuation** (what happens next)

### **Best Practices:**
- Always show timer/progress if time-limited
- Provide clear visual feedback for correct actions
- Never make failure state frustrating (allow retries)
- Use emoji liberally for accessibility & charm
- Keep single mini-game under 60 seconds
